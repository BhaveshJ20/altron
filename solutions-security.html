Identity

Workforce

It is a known fact that cyber-criminals are often able to break into secure environments by using social engineering techniques and employees' personal information shared on social media. It is for this reason we recommend biometric identification, as this delivers a far higher degree of security than manual processes such as passwords, e-mail addresses or personal identity numbers (PINs). Because biometrics utilise an individual's unique characteristics, such as fingerprints and voice, they not only offer robust security, but also free users from the problems of remembering passwords and PINs.

Our biometric verification and authentication solutions are able to curb fraud resulting from identity theft and misrepresentation, by supplying end-to-end biometric identity and access management hardware, along with software and consulting solutions that meet the needs for biometric enrolment, authentication and verification. Our reputation in this arena is such that we are a trusted partner to the Top five banks, as well as to both government and several large telecommunications companies in South Africa.

Just as imperative, security-wise, is the need to stay on top of the joiners, movers and leavers of your workforce. Identifying users and managing their access is ‘mission critical’ for Identity Security – because movers or leavers often cause scattered access legacies, entitlement creep or worse. We provide an Identity Governance solution that ensures that each of your employees has the appropriate ‘least privilege’ access. The new joiner process is automated as far as possible through a single user interface to select and approve access.these Identity Governance solutions address the challenges your company may experience in managing a diverse set of user account challenges.

DevSecOps

For a DevOps approach to remain sustainable, it needs to be secure and it is for this reason that the concept of DevSecOps has been brought to the fore - it allows for security to be built into the DevOps process, allowing application development to be undertaken rapidly, but nonetheless securely.

DevSecOps ensures the ongoing integration of security practices within the DevOps process, by focusing on creating new security solutions for complex software development processes. The goal here is to overcome the traditional gaps that are found between between IT and security, thereby securely enabling the rapid delivery of code.

Using this approach, you can eliminate the standard silo-type thinking around security, ensuring the shared responsibility of security tasks during all phases of the delivery process. With the implementation of DevSecOps, customers are assured of complete security throughout the process of development, testing, and deployment of apps.

Security

Edge

With the majority of applications and services moving into the cloud and now being accessed via the Internet, rather than a corporate network, it no longer makes sense to run security in a traditional, hosted, on-premise, centrally controlled way. This is the point where cyber-security intersects with the rise of the cloud.

To solve the challenges created by this shift, Altron has implemented a Secure Access Service Edge (SASE), where security services have been moved into the cloud and are now offered as a service. This brings security closer to both users and their applications and services - by placing it at the edge - and gives organisations the operational and budgetary agility and scalability to navigate changing circumstances, such as the switch to remote working.

With a SASE approach, your security controls live in the cloud and users access the SASE-based cloud security solution via the Internet, enabling it to act as a secure conduit to the rest of the world.

Altron also offers cloud-based solution for ID & bank card issuance that sits at the Edge, known as Identity as a service (IDAAS). This provides simultaneous physical and digital card issuance solutions to various market verticals, including Financial, Government, Retail, Loyalty, Healthcare and Access Control. IDaaS allows for the in-branch issuance of personalised debit and credit cards for certain financial services sector organisations.
